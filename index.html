A gaming cloud is a fully ready-to-play Sims FreePlay game account. Each cloud is different and unique in its in-game content — from collections of purchased online packs to exclusive event items and one-of-a-kind built houses not available anywhere else.

"All Game Clouds were originally created around 2012–2013 and have been actively built and enriched with content for 11–13 years 💕"
