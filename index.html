What Is a Gaming Cloud

A Gaming Cloud is a fully prepared Sims FreePlay game save, built over many years of active gameplay.

Each world contains a unique combination of:

• Online store packs
• Limited-time event items
• Exclusive buildings and custom houses
• High-level progression and VIP status

Some accounts are actively updated. Others are fully completed collections. All of them represent long-term gameplay value and rare in-game history.

Value & Purpose

A cloud gives instant access to a fully developed Sims FreePlay universe — without years of progression, collection, or spending.

Each account reflects approximately $6,000–$8,000+ in real in-game purchases, accumulated over years of gameplay.

What Defines Each Cloud

Pricing and structure are based on content depth:

• VIP level and player progression
• Size of online pack collection
• Rare and discontinued event items
• Exclusive pre-built buildings
• Active updates or completed status

Some clouds focus on full creative freedom. Others offer fully designed luxury cities ready to explore.

Pricing Principle

Higher-value clouds include:

✔ Larger and rarer collections
✔ More exclusive buildings
✔ Greater historical content
✔ Active updates (selected worlds)

Lower-priced clouds prioritize creative freedom with minimal structures, while still containing valuable collections.
